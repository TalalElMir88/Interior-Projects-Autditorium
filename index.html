Auditorium.

The project "Auditorium" presents a bold architectural vision by combining various interior styles in a harmonious blend. The diagram showcases a meticulous selection of spatial organization, material choices, lighting techniques, circulation patterns, and ergonomic considerations. This integration of diverse architectural elements creates a cohesive and sophisticated design approach that elevates the project's overall aesthetic and functionality.
